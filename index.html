Built by hand. Powered by solidarity.

BaBaLift is an open-source movement engineered to dismantle isolation and restore independent human connection across communities. We build multi-seat, adaptive tandem frameworks entirely FROM RECYCLED PARTS AND REUSED MATERIALS, proving that global resourcefulness can fuel profound social integration. This prototype setup is purposefully crafted to lift absolutely anyone—including individuals with disabilities, the blind, the elderly, and children.

Chapter I: Design & Geometry Planning

01

Every adaptive frame begins on standard graph paper, mapping exact weight distributions, front-fork rigidity ratios, and structural load metrics. Transitioning these blueprints into multi-color digital renders allows precise analysis of tube intersections and structural stress vectors.

Chapter II: Workshop & Fabrication

02

Individual chassis tube sections are measured and cut by hand inside standard workshops using recycled components. Fusing connections outdoors with stick welding units guarantees that the structural frame achieves the resilience required to navigate rugged infrastructure routes safely.

Chapter III: Community Integration & Operations

03

True mechanical validation happens out on public paths, logging kilometers that prove structural handling under real conditions while fostering spontaneous trust between co-riders.

About Us

Mission

To create inclusive, open-source mobility solutions that bring people together through shared journeys, freedom of movement and human connection.

Vision

A world where mobility is accessible, joyful and community-driven — built by people, for people.

Values

  • Solidarity over profit: Prioritizing human dignity and communal wellness.
  • Human connection through movement: Breaking down social boundaries.
  • Open-source creativity: Distributing technical blueprints freely.
  • Reuse and sustainability: Hand-crafting from discarded parts.
  • Universal inclusion: Tailoring geometry for everyone.

Cooperation

In the past 15 years we have cooperated with many realities including ELOI, Red Cross, Co.P.E., Mondo Sostenibile, and more.

Our main Partner is Anymore Onlus. Any donation is welcome, only, thru them at anymoreonlus.org.
