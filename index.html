BaBaLift Movement

Officially Aided by ANYMORE Onlus (Registered ETS)

Built by hand. Powered by solidarity.

BaBaLift is an open-source movement engineered to dismantle isolation through adaptive mobility. We specialize in building multi-seat tandem frameworks that facilitate Inclusive Tourism and Sustainable Travel experiences. By utilizing recycled components to craft high-capacity cycles, we enable community groups, families, and travelers of all abilities—including the blind, elderly, and those with disabilities—to explore their surroundings through guided, low-impact, human-powered journeys.

Core Goals & Impact

  • Promoting Sustainable Tourism: Developing human-powered, low-carbon mobility solutions for experiential travel and local tours.
  • Fostering Inclusive Community Tours: Creating accessible tandem cycles to ensure everyone can participate in outdoor excursions.
  • Open-Source Global Sharing: Distributing technical blueprints to empower local fabrication and social business models.
  • Skill-Building & Repair Training: Training local communities in mechanical maintenance and professional tour coordination.
