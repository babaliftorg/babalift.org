Built by hand. Powered by solidarity.

Inclusive Tourism and Sustainable Travel experiences:

  • Promoting Sustainable Tourism: Developing human-powered, low-carbon mobility solutions for experiential travel and local tours.
  • Fostering Inclusive Community Tours: Creating accessible tandem cycles to ensure everyone can participate in outdoor excursions.

BaBaLift is an open-source movement engineered to dismantle isolation and restore independent human connection across communities. We build multi-seat, adaptive tandem frameworks entirely FROM RECYCLED PARTS AND REUSED MATERIALS, proving that global resourcefulness can fuel profound social integration.
