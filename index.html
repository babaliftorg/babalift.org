WELCOME

Built by hand. Powered by solidarity.

BaBaLift is an open-source movement engineered to dismantle isolation and restore independent human connection across communities. We build multi-seat, adaptive tandem frameworks entirely FROM RECYCLED PARTS AND REUSED MATERIALS, proving that global resourcefulness can fuel profound social integration. This prototype setup is purposefully crafted to lift absolutely anyone—including individuals with disabilities, the blind, the elderly, and children. The balanced chassis architecture is uniquely responsive, ensuring that even kids can effortlessly take control in the pilot position or ride up front during open-air play configurations.

Chapter I: Design & Geometry Planning

01

Every adaptive frame begins on standard graph paper, mapping exact weight distributions, front-fork rigidity ratios, and structural load metrics. Transitioning these blueprints into multi-color digital renders allows precise analysis of tube intersections and structural stress vectors, creating replication paths that independent global builders can interpret easily using localized elements.

Chapter II: Workshop & Fabrication

02

Individual chassis tube sections are measured and cut by hand inside standard workshops using recycled components. Fusing connections outdoors with stick welding units guarantees that the structural frame achieves the resilience required to navigate rugged infrastructure routes safely. Specialized folding parameters are integrated directly to lower the storage profile, streamlining global shipping transitions.

Chapter III: Community Integration & Operations

03

True mechanical validation happens out on public paths, logging kilometers that prove structural handling under real conditions while fostering spontaneous trust between co-riders. Our first operational prototype successfully logged over 15,000 kilometers and provided more than 10,000 community rides to promote sport for peace. Today, this global footprint expands across active chassis paths in Africa, Europe, and our newly built initial prototype in India, which is already delivering vital shared experiences daily.

About Us

Mission

To create inclusive, open-source mobility solutions that bring people together through shared journeys, freedom of movement and human connection.

Vision

A world where mobility is accessible, joyful and community-driven — built by people, for people.

Values

  • Solidarity over profit: Prioritizing human dignity and communal wellness over financial structures.
  • Human connection through movement: Breaking down historical social boundaries via open-air co-riding environments.
  • Open-source creativity: Distributing technical blueprints freely to empower global local manufacturing.
  • Reuse and sustainability: Hand-crafting long-lasting transport frameworks directly from structural components and discarded parts.
  • Universal inclusion: Actively tailoring geometry balances to lift children, seniors, blind, and disabled co-pilots seamlessly.

Goals

  • Build affordable multi-seat bicycles using recycled materials
  • Support mobility for children and people with disabilities
  • Share open-source designs worldwide
  • Train local communities in fabrication and repair
  • Connect people through shared rides and collaborative journeys
  • Expand the project across Africa, Europe and Asia
